Divisions

Multiple divisions, determined by the rankings on TLS, and NTRP.


Season Play

  • Play each division opponent once.
  • You have 4 weeks to play the first 3 opponents. Then another 4 weeks to play the next 3 opponents, etc.
  • Recommended dates for matches are every Thursday at 7 PM. If both players agree to a different date and time, that’s just fine.
  • Recommended place is the PTC. If both players agree to a different location, that’s just fine.
  • You can play matches as far in advance as you like. For example, if you want to play your week 7 opponent in week 2, go right ahead. Just also make sure that you’re keeping up with your other opponents.
  • Home team supplies the tennis balls: one new can.
  • Same match scoring as the USTA league (two sets, tiebreakers, etc.)


Playoffs

Top four from each division play each other (1v4, 2v3). Then those winners play each other for the title.


Division Tiebreakers

Head-to-head will be used only in the event of a two-way tie. If there are three or more tied with the same win/loss record, then the tiebreakers are: sets won minus sets lost, then games won minus games lost.


Dropping Out

If someone drops out during the season, all the matches involving that player are erased. Please don’t drop out.
